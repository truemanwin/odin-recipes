Jake's most delicious sandwich

Jake's most delicious sandwich is, according to Jake, "the greatest sandwich ever made."

Jake required instruction by the realm of creation in order to know how the sandwich would be conceived.

Ingredients

  • Baguette
  • Cream cheese
  • Pickles (from Prismo)
  • Dill
  • Diced boiled eggs
  • Bird (from the window)
  • Sliced cucumbers
  • Sliced Roma tomatoes
  • Sweet yellow onions
  • Tears (for salt)
  • Meat prepared sous-vide with rosemary and thyme
  • Bacon
  • A lobster's soul

Steps

  1. Vacuum seal the meat with rosemary and thyme. Place it into a 135 degree sous vide.
  2. Now we start preparing the sandwich. Cut the baguette into halves and toast it accordingly (oven or blowtorch, your choice!)
  3. Spread cream cheese on both sides.
  4. Now on one side, sliced pickles from that boy Prismo, and some dill on the other side.
  5. "Yeah baby, now we're rolling," grab the nearest bird from a window, debone, and broil it respectively.
  6. Back to the sandwich, diced boiled eggs and the bird from the window.
  7. Top it with slices of common cucumbers, Roma tomatoes, and sweet yellow onions (organic of course).
  8. Almost done. Tears, salt to taste, the meat sous vide from earlier, and slices of bacon.
  9. For the most important part of all, lobster soul. Of course if you have make to adjustments, Babish has an excellent substitute solution using lobster aioli.
